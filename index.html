Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know before booking your buddy — no confusion, only clarity.

What kind of work does my buddy maid do? ▼

Your buddy maid handles all household work — sweeping, mopping, dusting, utensils, laundry, cooking, grocery organization, and more. For specialized buddies, they also handle baby care, elderly support, post-natal care, and deep cleaning. You define the duties, and we match accordingly!

How do you verify the maids? Is it safe? ▼

Safety is our topmost priority. Every buddy goes through police verification, Aadhaar & ID verification, past employer reference checks, and an in-person behavioral assessment. We share the complete verification file with you before deployment. 100% safe, zero compromise.

What if my maid leaves or I'm not satisfied? ▼

No tension at all! We offer a free replacement guarantee — 90 days for Silver, 180 for Gold, and a full 365 days for Diamond. If your buddy leaves or isn't the right fit, we send another one within 48 hours at absolutely zero extra cost.

How much does a buddy maid cost per month? ▼

Monthly salary depends on your city, work profile, and hours. Part-time starts from ₹5,000/month in Tier-2 cities and ₹8,000 in metros. Full-time live-in buddies range from ₹12,000-₹25,000/month. Use our Salary Estimator tool above for instant estimates! The one-time placement fee is equal to one month's salary.

How quickly can I get a buddy maid? ▼

Super quick! For Diamond package, we prioritize deployment within 24 hours. For Gold and Silver, your buddy arrives within 2-5 working days. In urgent cases, call us directly and we'll try our best to fast-track requests.

Do you provide maids in my city? ▼

We currently serve 25+ cities across India including all major metros (Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, Kolkata) and Tier-2 cities (Jaipur, Lucknow, Indore, Chandigarh, and more). Check the cities section above or call us — we're expanding every month!

What is the Japan-style postnatal buddy service? ▼

Inspired by Japan's legendary postnatal care culture, our premium postnatal buddies are specially trained in newborn hygiene, infant massage, lactation support, nutritious post-delivery cooking, and complete mother recovery care. It's the ultimate support for new moms who deserve to rest, heal, and bond with their baby.
